## 📈 How to Use Prompts Effectively

### 1. Be Specific
❌ "Write about AI"
✅ "Write a 500-word blog post about AI automation for small businesses, targeting non-technical founders"

### 2. Provide Context
❌ "Give me ideas"
✅ "I'm a freelance graphic designer with 3 years experience. Give me 10 content ideas for LinkedIn that attract high-paying clients"

### 3. Set Constraints
❌ "Write an email"
✅ "Write a 150-word follow-up email to a client who hasn't replied in 2 weeks. Tone: friendly but professional. Goal: Get meeting scheduled"

### 4. Ask for Format
❌ "Give me a plan"
✅ "Create a 30-day content calendar for Instagram in a table format with: Day, Post Type, Topic, Caption Idea, Hashtags"
